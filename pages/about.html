About the Attorney

Neil S. Ruskin

Brooklyn Criminal Defense Attorney · Former Prosecutor · Over 30 Years of Experience

A Career Dedicated to Criminal Defense

Neil S. Ruskin has spent over three decades fighting for the rights of the accused in Brooklyn and throughout New York City. His career began on the other side of the courtroom as a prosecutor, where he gained invaluable insight into how criminal cases are built, investigated, and tried.

That experience shaped everything about how he practices defense. Neil understands the prosecution's playbook because he once ran it. He knows where cases are strong, where they are weak, and how to exploit gaps that other attorneys might miss.

Why Clients Choose Neil Ruskin

When you hire Neil, you work with Neil. Not a junior associate. Not a paralegal handling the important decisions. He takes a limited number of cases so that each client gets the time, attention, and preparation their case demands.

His approach is direct: understand the facts thoroughly, identify every weakness in the prosecution's case, and build the strongest possible defense. Whether that means negotiating a favorable resolution or taking the case to trial, the strategy is always tailored to the client's best interest.
