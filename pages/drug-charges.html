Drug Charges Defense

Possession, intent to sell, trafficking, and search-and-seizure challenges in Brooklyn and NYC courts.

Drug Cases Often Turn On Police Conduct

Many drug prosecutions rise or fall on whether the stop, search, warrant execution, or seizure was lawful. These are detail-heavy cases where early review matters.

Common Defense Pressure Points

  • Illegal vehicle or street stops
  • Improper search warrants
  • Lack of possession or knowledge
  • Weak chain of custody and lab proof

Strategic Defense From The Start

Neil Ruskin examines how evidence was found, who actually controlled it, and whether prosecutors can prove possession, sale intent, or usable quantity beyond a reasonable doubt.
